Game Overview

Nanika Game Online is an innovative online falling block puzzle game featuring 66 diverse Nanika, 6 unique classes, and a variety of game modes. Experience strategically rich gameplay and aim for the top alongside your friends.

Nanika

Nanika Game Online features 66 diverse Nanika. Each Nanika possesses unique skills and evolves into a powerful form through fusion. Players create decks by combining Nanika across 11 levels, unlocking a wide range of strategic possibilities with unique combinations.

Classes

Six unique classes support your strategy. Make split-second decisions based on the situation and activate your skills at the perfect moment to gain the upper hand in battle.

Alchemist
